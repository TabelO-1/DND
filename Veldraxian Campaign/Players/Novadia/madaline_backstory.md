Novadia the Mighty lived in a city, s small one, alongside her mother who is obsessed with making her suceed and a father who gave her the title **Mighty**, always pushing her to newer lengths.

After quite a few years with much training and preperation, Novadia ventured out with her father on a sort of quest. Despite her doubts about her own strength, and her love for any and EVERY creature, she was determined to prove herself MIGHTY! And so when they got ambushed in the dead of night, she fought hard and decently well. But trouble started when she had to kill the captain of the ambush.

Upon hesitation, she got knocked out and when she awoke her father was nowhere to be seen and her village was nearly decimated. When she returned to her village, she was exiled and sent to the outer world to fend for herself.

After quite a few months of wandering, she encountered a traveller who told her about the Quests and campaigning. One story caught her attention, of of somebody named the Grandfather. And so with new motivation, (and quite a few chickenings out) she was on her way to trully become MIGHTY!
