### Character Summary: Aegis
Travelling Scholar who works for the Rich and Nobles doing whatever jobs they require in order to gain access to wealths of knowledge they have. Aegis seeks knowledge to best understand his magicraft and his draconic background. He knows more of his magic, but he doesn't well understand his draconic ancestry, he knows one parent who raised him but is still seeking answers as to his draconic ancestry. 

His connection with the Grandfather is that of another Scholar. When he was younger and newer in his search he met the Grandfather, who was a wealth of knowledge that aided Aegis in furthering his understand of magic, his ancestry, and how he approached learning his craft. After they parted the Grandfather has consistently helped him find jobs under the Rich, Nobles, or other Scholars to aid in furthering his knowledge.
