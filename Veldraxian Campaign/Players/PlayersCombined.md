### Character Summary: Shadow
Fullname: "Shadow Drifting at the Edge of Dawn"

After becoming an adult, Shadow naturally shifted toward the criminal underworld. That's where he'd eventually find himself leading a small gang of smugglers along the Veldrun River. He had met Quenlith, leading her own smuggling gang, and were tentatively peaceful. That was until he heard that she had a big score, and he decided to take the score for himself instead of allowing his rival to get ahead of him. The Operation went south fast, as the two gangs fought, the Grandfather appeared. Their two gangs had caught the attention of the authorities, but had proved elusive, however the authorities in Highriver had hired the Grandfather to take care of the issue, who was towards the end of his adventuring days.

Shadow was arrested, along with both of the gangs being scattered. Quenlith was able to flee. The Grandfather, knowing the Tabaxi's curious nature, he stated his case to the authorities that had hired him, requesting to take Shadow into his care. They obliged and granted his request, with Shadow being eternally grateful (saved from being a cat in a cage) and wished to learn more about the Grandfather and the reasons for his kindness. They travelled around for a few months more before the Grandfather took in Quenlith too, and not long after Tolin as well. They would then travel south to Greenrest, to the Grandfather's home.

Ultimately after a few months, the Grandfather suggested Shadow go to the Monastery south of Greenrest and become a monk, which he would, learning a better way of life and new combat skills. That was fifteen years ago. Shadow would end up keeping in touch with both the Grandfather and Quenlith.

### Character Summary: Novadia the Mighty (Nova)

Novadia the Mighty was raised in a dragonborn city to the far north. Nestled deep in the Crownfrost Region against the Northern Mountains, far from the road but not so far the city was cut off from the world.

Her father would give her the name **"Mighty"**, constantly pushing her toward strength, skill, and greatness. He believed greatly in her and trained her relentlessly, always demanding more from her than she ever believed she could give.

Her mother, by contrast, demanded success. Not strength for its own sake, but proof that Nova could become someone worthy, and someone respected. She ensured that whatever Nova did, she would do so sucessfully, almost obsessedly.

Between them, Nova grew into someone who tried desperately to live up to a name she was never sure she deserved. Despite her doubts, she was already more formidable than most. She has chosen the path of the Cleric, serving *Lliira, Goddess of Joy*, having attuned to her due to her love of any and all creatures. Serving under Lliira has some side effects. Joy comes easily to her, as do smiles. When she was finally deemed ready, her father took her out into the world. A quest she was to aid him in.

This is where everything would go wrong, and how everything would end in failure. Her and her father were ambushed in the night. Nova fought well, but when the moment came to kill, she hesitated, and in that moment of hesitation, her foe, the Captain of the Brigands struck her and she fell unconscious.

When she awoke, her father was gone. She could see fire on the horizon, and when she rushed back to her home, it had been left decimated in the wake of the attack. Once the villagers learned she was there, they gathered and exiled her. Leaving a child to fend for herself in the world as a punishment for her failure.

Since then, Nova has wandered, unsure, afraid, but still trying. Along the road, she would hear of a man known as the Grandfather, and his story would catch her attention. It took time for her to work up the courage, but she would choose to follow in his path of the adventurer, and continue her father's quest to become truly mighty.
