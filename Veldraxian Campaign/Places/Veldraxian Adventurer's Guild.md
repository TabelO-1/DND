**The Veldraxian Adventurer's Guild**: The Adventurer's Guild holds modest of notable influence throughout the Kingdom, maintaining a guildhall in all the major cities. Through these halls, adventurer's are guided toward work that serves the needs of the realm, and its people. Whether escort jobs, exploration, or dealing with threats beyond the scope of the ordinary folk.

Renown System in use: DM's Guide page 22.

# Renown Ranks:

## Prestige Ranks:

(S+): Platinum Rank
(S): Gold Rank
(A): Silver Rank 
(B): Iron Rank
(C): Bronze Rank
(D): Copper Rank
(E): Provisional Rank

Guild Missions or other recognized commissions increase Prestige Ranks
