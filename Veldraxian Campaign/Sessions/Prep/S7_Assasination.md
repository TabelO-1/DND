A shout of pain, then struggle, followed by an explosion. "A sudden burst of flames from the direction of the room."

-- Party Leave their rooms --

They see one person running down the stairs, leaving, and as they turn they see that Aeris' door has is open. And you hear the sounds of struggle coming from it.

-- Battle --

Two gangsters leave Aeris' room. One is injured, burnt, and hair slightly still on fire. The other seems fine
*Initiative Time*

Two Dagger Wielding Guys. AC 11, +1 to Stats, Injured has 6 HP, Other 12. 1d4+2 to hit with daggers.

-- Aeris' Room --

A side table near the door has been blown to smittereens, and the entrance is slightly on fire. Two gangsters lie dead on the floor, from fire magic. 
Aeris lays on the floor, blood pooling from his midsection, a dagger in his gut. 

He speaks his last words, "...they thought I saw...something..." He raise his hand, as if to point, before it drops to the ground.

-- After / Investigation --

If corpses are inspected: A capital "B" is tattooed into each persons' right hand. One Corpse in the room has some kind of ring with an insignia (A "B" with one star), a DC 10 investigation check will reveal that it means this person is some kind of low-level leader. He also has a note that says "Yellow Wyvern, Bronze Dragonborn. Tonight. Take who you need. Report East when finished."

-- Guard Arrival --

A few minutes after the Stonebridge Guard will burst in, 6 Guards and a Squad Leader. The guard will have their weapons drawn, one will step forward, "Drop your weapons! Step back, now!"

If the party resists, the guard will attempt to restrain them, otherwise they will be held for questioning, then released.

The Squad Leader will demand to know what happened, "What happened here?". 

During the Guard's investigation, one of the Guards, inspecting the corpses, will say "Brill Gang again, typical."
