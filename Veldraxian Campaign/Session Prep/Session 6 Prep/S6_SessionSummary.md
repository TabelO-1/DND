# Session Six

After exploring through Stonebridge, the party reconvened at the Adventurer's Guildhall, gathering once more around the quest board to decide their next job.

As Shadow and Tolin debated their options, the discussion was abruptly interrupted by the sudden sound of rapid footsteps. Before everyone could even fully turn around, a young blue-purple dragonborn, **Novadia the Mighty**, rushed across the hall and stopped directly in front of the group, where she demanded that she be allowed to join both the party and the job they were about to take.

What followed was an entertaining conversation. Shadow maintained his usual level of composure, drawing on techniques he learned from his time at the monastery. Following some back and forth, the party would agree to allow Novadia to join. Despite her intensity, excitability, and tendency to declare herself "Mighty", there were no concerns about her ability to contribute.

With the party's newest member included, the party returned to the matter at hand. Tolin argued in favor of taking the negotiation job, wanting to "Lawyer", while Novadia seemed hesitant about facing the bears, but would still half heartedly declare that she would be Mighty and face the bears. In the end, following Shadow's lead, the party would take on the procurement contract, purchasing and delivering barrels of apples on behalf of the *Ilhert Merchant's Guild*.

After confirming the details with the Merchant Guild, the Party departed for Port Valen. The journey passed without incident, aside from Tolin spending the three day trip in what could only be described as a prolonged, unexplained coma.

Upon arrival in Port Valen, the party secured lodging at a well-regarded establishment recommended by Shadow: *The Unicorns and Rainbows Tavern*. While there, they were issued a comically oversized key, which would then reveal a smaller key on the front once the handle was turned to open the room. An ingenius way to prevent travelers stealing room keys if I do say so myself.

While in the city, Shadow handled the logistics of the contract, meeting with the *Kaldrom Shipping Guild*, where the apples were to be acquired and going about purchasing a long cart and two donkeys to pull it, which required a temporary pooling of the party's funds.

During the stay, the party was busy. Quenlith would discover her tendency to sharpen arrows in her sleep. Nova, seeing the ocean for the first time, quickly learned that the ocean could and would drown you. Tolin, in a strange pattern, chose a bed over a table once more. Perhaps her days of table-sleeping were over.

With the goods secured by Shadow, the party returned to Stonebridge without issue and completed the delivery. The party recieved payment in full, 100 gp disturbed back to the party.

The Sparrow Fledging's first contract as a five member party: Complete.

After exploring around the town again the party convened back at the Guildhall and Questboard to choose a job. However in the middle of the arguement between Shadow and Tolin on which job to take, you all suddenly heard a noise, and turned to see Novadia bolt across the room at maximum speed to stop in front of you and demanded to join the party and the chosen quest.

After an entertaining discussion involving Shadow using some mental calming techniques he learned at his monastery to remain as calm as he was the party agreed to allow Novadia to join, as there seemed to be no issues with her qualifications to aid the party, despite her intensity, excitability, and tendencies to declare that she would be "Mighty". 

After which a quest was to be chosen. Tolin desired to "Lawyer" at the negotiation quest, Novadia seemed to not want to take on the bears quest, despite saying she would prove her Mightiness. Shadow however decided it would be best to take the quest to purchase Apples for a Merchant Guild.

After going to the *Ilhert Merchant Guild*, and confirming the details of the quest the Party would depart for Port Valen to complete the quest. There were no issues on the road except for Tolin spending three days in an apparent coma. 

Once arriving in Port Valen, the Party would stop at a reputable tavern recommended by Shadow, ***The Unicorns and Rainbows Tavern***. At the tavern the party would be handed a comically large key for their room, which turned would reveal a smaller key at the end used to open their door. An interesting method to stop travellers leaving with the room keys indeed.

Shadow would handle the business of purchasing a long cart and donkeys to pull it, requiring a temporary donation of funds from the party to pick up apples from the *Kaldrom Shipping Guild*. During this time Quenlith would learn of her tendency to sharpen her arrows in her sleep, Novadia, whom had never seen the ocean would learn that the ocean could and would drown those who could not swim. Tolin would seem to be overcoming her need to sleep on tables, having spend the last few nights sleeping on a bed.

After the second night, the Party would recieve the merchandise and return to Stonebridge, no trouble on the road again. After the delivery of the goods, the party would recieve payment of 100 gp for completion of the quest.

"First" Quest is Complete
