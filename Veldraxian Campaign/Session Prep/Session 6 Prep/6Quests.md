# Basic Details

### Escort to Lakehold
Details
Escort Three Carts to Lakehold

Two Day Trip. Stopping in Brumbleford for the night.
Pay: 40 GP
Reputation: +2 Guild Renown

### Escort to Greenwatch
Details
Escort Two Carts to Greenwatch Crossing

Two Day Trip. Walking through the night to arrive at The Crossroads, and then arriving at Greenwatch the second ay.

Pay: 50 GP
Reputation: +2 Guild Renown

### Negotiations
Details
Stonebridge -

Negotiate for the Western People's Union on the price of charcoal at the Ilhert Merchant's Guild.

Guild Asking Price: 1sp per Bundle of Charcoal
Union Wants as low as possible, but will settle for any price decrease.

Pay: 25 GP per cp lowered
Reputation: +3 Renown, +7 Reputation with the Western People's Union, +3 With Stonebridge General Populace

### Debt Collection
Details
Stonebridge

On behalf of the Brill Gang Operating out of the Eastern Residental Sector, extort a shop owner into paying his dues.

Pay: 35 GP
Reputation: +1 Guild Renown, +3 with the Brill Gang, -3 Stonebridge General Populace

### Purchase Apple Barrels
Details
Go to Port Valen

On behalf of the Ilhert Merchant's Guild, purchase 30 Barrels of Apples from a shipping guild in Port Valen. They will provide the 90 GP as funds for the Apples. The party is responsible for not losing those funds, and of course, if they run off with them, the Guild will alert the authorities.

Three day trip, camping out.

Pay: 100 GP
Reputation: +3 Guild Renown, +2 with Ilhert Merchant Guild

### Bear Clearing Mission
Details
Go to Hollowmere

One day trip there. The Residents report a number of bears in the area attacking livestock.

Pay: 60 GP
Reputation: +2 Guild Renown, +5 with Hollowmere General Populace

# How it Goes

Escort Quest to Lakehold: Without Issue
Escort Quest to Greenwatch Crossing: Ambush. 6 Bandits Attack. Stats: Guard -4 AC, -3 HP. 3 Attack from both sides, two archers, 4 front line.
Negotiations: Can use dice rolls, but would prefer to RP it out. If dice rolls: a nat 20 will reduce price by 4cp, 18/19 by 3, 13-17 by 2, 11-12 by 1, 10 and lower no go.
Debt Collection: The man will need to be intimidated. He will not be too willing, and depending on character actions may require a few punches to get him to give in. He will be nervous, but also state he already paid this month, and that they were double charging him.
Purchase Apple Barrels: No issue, just time and money. Also they will have to figure out how to move 30 Barrels of apples. Ilhert Merchant Guild will ask: How do you intent to transport them?
Bear Clearing: 3 Bears attack the party about 500 ft from the village. If the PCs track correctly and use stealth, they can get a suprise round.
