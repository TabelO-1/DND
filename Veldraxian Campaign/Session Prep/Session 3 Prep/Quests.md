Escort Quest: Merchant Looking for an Escort North. Pays 50 gp for the party to get him to Stonebridge. Leaves in two days.

Guard Expedition: Two Guards will Accompany the Party South to search for the Goblin Camp. Upon finding it: 12 Goblins and 1 Leader. Can simply report back and get paid, but extra can be gained from taking them out there. There will be sentries patrolling around the Camp. Two Goblins per patrol, Four out at a time. Can be killed. Pay is 50 gp for intel, 150 for killing the goblins.

Clear Wolves: Half a Day's Travel West they will find the wolves. 5 Wolves will Attack. Pay is 50gp
