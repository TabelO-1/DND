*Quests* <br> The Escort Quest gives 2 Guild Reputation, the Wolves 1, and the Goblin camp gives 1 for finding and 3 for clearing.

### Escort

Merchant: Rennic (Vale)
Apprentice: Logan (Vale)

He has two carts of timber from the village, long carts pulled by several horses. He has an apprentice with him to steer the second. He's leaving today. If convinced, he will say "I can delay a day, no more."

**Roadside Encounter**: Two Merchants arguing over a broken axle on their cart.

Ahead on the road: One stopped card with a broken wheel, two travelers arguing. you can't see behind the upcoming hill. The Merchant tenses.

Outcomes: Simple assistance, help them with the wheel. Nothing. Suspicion: Are they bandits?
These are actually just merchants, down on their luck for not packing a spare wheel.

If aided: 1 Guild Reputation Increase (Decide on values for ranks later)

**Wolves**

This section of the forest Quenlith knows well, its near her cabin. 5 Wolves to fight, if perception checks fail the wolves will get a surprise round attacking from all sides. If detected the party will be ready for it.

If 3 die, the other two flee. 30 GP Pay

**Goblin Camp (Hard Quest)**

Two Guards will accompany them. Their goals: Find the Goblin Camp (Pay 20 GP), clearing the camp: 100 GP. There are 13 Goblins in total. Ideally the players will not just rush into a hard battle. 4 Goblins patrol at a time, in two groups of two. The patrol will swtich, giving the players chance to elimate up to 8 goblins. Leaving 4 and the leader. If they are too loud, the goblins will detect them.

## Stonebridge

IF we get to Stonebridge because we just skip past everyone: A large town, maybe 10000 residesnts. You cross the stonebridge (over the Valenreach River) the town is named after, come past patrols of Guards on the way in, a long line awaits entrance for carts. The town has tall stone walls that are easily 40 feet tall. Looking back south across the Valenreach River you can see the Standing Stone Mesas hundreds of feet into the air, North of the City is the Elderward, with trees almost a Hundred Feet tall even at the forest's border, residents will whisper of people getting lost in there, never to be seen again.

The Stonebridge Guildhall makes the GWC one look tiny. Many adventurer's travel through this area, and you can clearly see a number of adventurer's whom look considerably stronger than you, alongside a large number of ones whom look more similar to your level. Entering it and registering will require a wait of about half an hour. The registration process is the same as above.
