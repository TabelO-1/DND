# Session Four

With your registration complete, each of you received your Guild Cards, steel tokens bearing the name **Sparrow Fledgings**, your Provisional Rank, with Shadow recognized as the party's leader.

Your first job was decided soon after, an escort job. The Merchant, Rennic Vale, required an protective escort for his goods on the road to Stonebridge, offering 50 gold pieces for a safe arrival.

The journey began north at noon that same day. Two wagons loaded with timber, Rennic in the lead cart, and his apprentice Logan in the second set out north. The Party took their positions across the wagons, Tolin notably forbidden from riding with Rennic after her previous actions (much to Logan's misfortune). 

Travel was steady, uneventful, and by nightfall you reached a small settlement known as the Crossroads, where two roads meet, one continuing north to Stonebridge, the other turning west to the Elven Kingdom.  After a night's rest you continued onwards.

As the road stretched onwards, the the towering mesas came into view. At first they only reached a few hundred feet into the air as sheer cliffs, ascending straight into the sky. However, as you got closer, their true scale became apparent, some reaching miles high, jutting straight out of the ground, as if thrust straight out of the Earth. Their presence loomed over the journey long after they first appeared. You made camp once more before the final stretch, taking turns on watch through the night.

On the third and final day of the journey you came upon a stopped wagon right before a hillcrest. Two merchants argued beside it, locked in a heated arguement over the cart's broken axle, both seemingly neither willing nor able to solve the problem. Rather than leave them stranded to continue the fist fight Tolin interupted, the party devised a solution, securing the damaged cart with rope and dragging it behind the rear cart to Stonebridge.

By the time you reached Stonebridge, the scale of the city felt small compared to the towering Mesas you passed, but it was still a major city. Long lines of carts waited at the gates, under tall stone walls. After passing through, Rennic led the group to the Merchant's Quarter, stopping by a Merchant Guild where he conducted his business, selling his goods and paying you in full for the sucessful job.

Before parting ways, he informed you of where he was staying, a tavern known as the Winged Unicorn. Your first job has been completed.
