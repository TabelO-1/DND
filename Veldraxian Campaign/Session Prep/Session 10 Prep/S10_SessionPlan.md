Guard Involvement

If guards are present, they hang back unless there is open violence. Afterward, Eldid can secure prisoners and evidence. This helps keep the party from becoming murder suspects.

If the party excludes the guards, they have more freedom but more risk. If they leave bodies, witnesses, or fire, the guards may arrive after and demand answers. This is not punishment; it is city consequence.

Session End Points

Best ending: the party finds the ledger and realizes Aeris’ death was part of something organized.

Second best ending: the lieutenant escapes with or without the ledger, leaving Filen Warehouse or Marlo as the next lead.

Emergency ending: if time runs short, end as they reach the Wolf Tavern and see the fake pub under the dim evening light.

Mental anchor for the session: this is not “bring down the Brill Gang.” This is “prove the Brill Gang is larger than one room.”
