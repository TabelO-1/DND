# Session Seven
*Murder in the Tavern*

After completing their latest job, the party returned to the Yellow Wyvern Tavern to rest. Aeris chose to retire early, taking a room to himself - perhaps qisely, given the company he kept. The rest of the group lingered downstairs. Drinks were had, while Novadia enjoyed a comically large size of apple cider, the evening would come to a close.

It did not remain quiet for long.

In the middle of the night, the party was jolted awake by the sound of violent banging against a door. A struggle followed, muffled at first, then unmistakable. A shout, a crash of breaking glass, then a sudden burst of flame as the door to Aeris' room was blown open.

Aeris' cried out "HELP!"

Shadow moved first, crossing the hallway in an instant and intercepted two figures fleeing the room. Quenlith caught sight of another attacker already escaping down the stairs. Novadia peeked into the hall, weapon in hand, while Tolin, having heavily indulged in alcohol the evening more, stumbled from her room, only partially aware of what was going on.

The fight was short. Shadow struck hard, sending one attacker crashing back into the room while leaving the other barely standing. Quenlith loosed an arrow that embedded itself in the wall inches from the man's head. Tolin, in a moment that defied both logic and expectation, charged forward wielding a pillow and striking with a level of precision that suggested a lifetime experience in pillow-based combat. Novadia ended it, bringing her mace down on the man's head.

Everyone flooded into Aeris' room. Two more attackers lay dead, burned by Aeris' magic. And on the floor as well, was Aeris. A dagger had been driven into his gut, blood pooled beneath him.

"They thought I saw...something..." were his final words.

---

Shadow would send the others back into their rooms before heading downstairs. It was not long before the Stonebridge Guard arrived in force alongside the barkeep. After securing the scene and gathering the party, they indentified the attackers as members of the **Brill Gang**, a criminal group known to operate throughout the Eastern Districts of the city.

The party was released after questioning, and informed that if they could think of anything more, to report to a the Officer that arrived on scene named Eldid at any of the city's towers.

Morning came with little rest. The Tavern was nearly empty. Word of the attack had spread quickly and most patrons had chosen not to visit that morning. While the others remained for breakfast, Shadow may his way to the nearest Guard Tower to speak with Eldid. There, he learned more.

The Brill Gang has operated in Stonebridge for nearly a decade, centered around Brill, a former adventurer. They had steadily expanded their influence until they effectively controled most of the Eastern Residental Sector. They were identified by a capital B tattooed into their right arms. Eldid also mentioned a runner named Drud, though he doubted the man would have much useful to say.

Rejoining Novadia and Quenlith, Shadow made his way towards the Guildhall. There, among the posted jobs, he found something of interest, a request from the Brill Gang - an extortion job disguised as debt collection.

Before investigating further, Tolin made her presence known by startling him, resulting in him reacting by a reflexive kick to the face. Angered, she retaliated in the only way that would make sense for her: By starting an impromptu performance. A sea shanty echoed through the Guildhall out of this former pirate's lips. She was quickly joined by Novadia, playing an instrument, while Quenlith revealed that she was also a tap dancer ("It's canon now").

As people settled around, joining in this impromptu party, Shadow met with Marrek, who was understandably confused as to the commotion. In his office, he informed him of Aeris' death, and recieved a letter from the Grandfather. When asked about the Brill, Marrek mentioned a well-known runner for the gang, Drud, a not very bright one.

With that, Shadow returned to the imprompty party/performance, informing Quenlith of his intent to return to the Guard Tower.

With that, Session 7 is concluded.
