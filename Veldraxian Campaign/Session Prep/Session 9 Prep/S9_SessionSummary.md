# Session Nine
*"I didn't mean to kill him guys"*

The session began inside the Guard Tower near Stonebridge's Military Quarter, where the party was quickly cleared of any wrongdoing following Eldid's arrival and investigation into the day's events.

With Drud and the remaining Brill Gang member still unconscious, Shadow explained everything that had happened so far. Once Drud finally awoke, Eldid allowed the party to participate in his interrogation.

Drud soon found himself in an extremely uncomfortable position. Six people crowded into a small cell around him, all staring expectantly at him after he had just suffered a thorough beating at the paws of a monk moving far faster than he thought possible.

Tolin repeatedly suggested torture as a solution, which Eldid repeatedly shut down. Fortunately, Shadow took a different approach.

He convinced Drud that he was currently going to take the blame for everything the Brill Gang had done up to now.

Drud admitted to know little about Aeris' murder, only that there had been a problem, and it had already been handled. However, he did reveal the location of one of the gang's safehouses: **The Wolf Tavern**.

Following a brief discussion with Eldid, Shadow chose to keep the guards involved in the investigation. Eldid and another guard would accompany the party, allowing them to take the lead while providing official support if necessary.

---

By late evening, the party arrived on Stonebridge's eastern side. The Wolf Tavern immediately stood out.

Despite the fading sign hanging outside, there was no music, no laughter, no sounds of conversation or mugs clinking together. Curtains covered every window, and nobody entered or left. It looked exactly what it was: a place pretending to be a tavern.

Shadow began circling the building to scout, while Tolin and Novadia, who were quickly becoming quite the pair whenever questionable ideas were involved, hid beside the front entrance in an attempt to peek through the windows.

Then disaster struck. Or rather, Tolin struct disaster.

A Brill Gang member opened the front door, and before anyone could determine his intentions, Tolin rushed around the corner, and buried a dagger in his chest, killing him instantly.

Her immediate response: **"I didn't mean to kill him guys!"** She then threw the same dagger into the tavern, but missed.

Shadow, who had accepted this as a normal occurrence by now, simply continued scouting around the back of the building.

Quickly, chaos erupted. Quenlith, having apparently rediscovered her confidence with a bow, planted an arrow directly between one gangster's eyes. Novadia rush in, vaulting over the bar and slammed the other in the face with her shield.

Dazed and panicking by the sudden intrusion that took place in a matter of seconds, that gangster made the unfortunate decision to flee by diving through a nearby window.

He landed outside next to Shadow, he did not get back up. Turns out glass is sharp.

The party entered the building, followed shortly by Eldid and the accompanying guard, who had still not managed to participate in any of the fighting.

As they stood in the Tavern, two more gang members emerged from the back door.

Tolin engaged one while Quenlith, in an impressive display of coordination, immediately tripped over a chair before sucessfully firing an arrow into the other's chest.

Novadia charged in once more, smashing the injured man with her shield, driving the arrow deeper, killing him as she declared herself Mighty.

Meanwhile Tolin was stabbed during her duel with the other, which made retaliating significantly more difficult. Quenlith rushed forward, shortsword in hand, only for her opponent to kick it from her hand and call for reinforcements.

At the same time, Shadow had reached the rear entrance, bashed down the back door, and sent the unfortunate guard stationed there fleeing for his life.

Shadow chased him down with a speed they never see coming.

Two additional gang members appeared from another room in the hallway. Novadia charged in yet again, this time slamming one of them so hard with her shield that he collapsed unconscious.

Or as she loudly declared, **"I unconscioused one!"**

The other gang member immediately fled out the back door, only to chose to run left...directly into the returning Shadow. It was not a successful escape attempt.

Finally, Quenlith drew her second shortsword with her left hand and finished the last opponent.

Throughout all of this, Eldid and the accompanying guard remained stuck behind the party, unable to squeeze through the narrow hallway without getting in the way. The Stonebridge Guard officially participated in the raid by observing.

---

Quick Summary

The party interrogated Drud, learned of a Brill Gang safehouse known as the Wolf Tavern, and chose to work alongside Eldid and the Stonebridge Guard.

Upon arriving at the safehouse, Tolin immediately killed the first person she saw, accidentally beginning an all-out assault on the building. The party swiftly dismantled the operation, defeating numerous gang members while the guards mostly stood nearby and watched due to the confined space inside the building.

The Brill Gang's foothold in Stonebridge has begun to crack.
